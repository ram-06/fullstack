As this writing scholarship contest continues to grow rapidly, Writers’ Square is taking a hiatus in 2020. During this time, the organization plans to adjust the scholarship program to enhance our support for the contest and ensure that we can provide more comprehensive services for everyone in the future. The website and our contest will be paused starting on January 1, 2020. Apologies for the inconvenience.
