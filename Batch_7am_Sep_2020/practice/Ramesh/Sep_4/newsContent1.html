News Item:1

The Indian Army on Tuesday dismissed reports claiming 'infiltration attempt by Chinese Army along LAC in Ladakh's Chumar' as false. In a statement, the Army clarified that it was routine peacetime activities on China's side of the LAC and "the same cannot be inferred as an intrusion attempt".
