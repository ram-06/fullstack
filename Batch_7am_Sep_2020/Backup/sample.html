Todays Special Edition news....

News Details 1:

Amid the ongoing tense standoff at Pangong Tso along the Line of Actual Control (LAC) in eastern Ladakh, the Indian Army had recently made some more ‘readjustments’ and taken back control of heights overlooking the Chinese Army positions at Finger 4. Now, the proof is out and Indian Army bases can be clearly seen on the Finger 4 height. A satellite image released by OSINT Twitter handle d-atis @detresfa_ shows Indian Army post located at a height of 5256 metres. The Indian Army Post overlooks the Chinese Army post on the Finger 4 ridgeline, located 900 metres downhill. Click here to visit our main page and continue reading
